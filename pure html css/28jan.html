About Doggo World

The Doggo World is run by highly experienced and qualified veterinarians and paramedical staff under the able guidance of Dr.Mansi Sharma, the specialist in Canine Clinical Medicine, Pet Grooming, Pet Nutrition and Behavior. Doggy World is situated in Rohini, Delhi, and is one of its kinds in whole India catering to all needs of a dog at a single point.


The Doggo World provides an array of veterinary services ranging from preventive health care, general health management, treatment, surgery, vaccination, dentistry, pathology, ultrasonography, X-ray etc. The clinic ensures high recovery rate. During treatment the handling of pets is done is most lovingly and humane manner.


At Doggo World, we dedicate ourselves to give best therapies to the pets so that they can be treated in the most effective way. We are committed to reducing the suffering of the pets coming to our clinic.
